Our Story

Founded in 2023 in Nairobi, KejaSmart was born out of a frustration with the traditional rental management process. Our founder, James Mwangi, struggled to manage his rental properties efficiently while working a full-time job.

After months of missed payments, maintenance delays, and paperwork headaches, James envisioned a platform that would simplify rental management for both landlords and tenants. He assembled a team of passionate developers and property experts, and KejaSmart was launched in early 2024.

Today, we serve thousands of landlords and tenants across Kenya, helping them manage their rental relationships with ease, transparency, and efficiency.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To simplify rental management through innovative technology, making it effortless for landlords to manage properties and for tenants to enjoy seamless rental experiences.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's leading rental management platform, transforming how rental properties are managed and setting new standards for transparency and efficiency in the real estate industry.
